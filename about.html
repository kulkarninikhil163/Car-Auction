About Us

CarRebel.com is another site among excellent car websites that promises a large selection of vehicles from which to choose & it is a membership-only car auction site. It also connects buyers with auctions throughout the US and around the world. It is open to members from car dealers.

The basic idea behind this was to have the customers ease in buying a car at cheap rates by auctioning on it. The business started in 2015. The website works as the auctioneer and bidder process. The auctioneer put his/her automotive for auction & the bidder can bid on the automative where he/she finds best suits for him/her by having a look towards the model and all other details. Hence, you must be a dealership/licensed dealer to sign up.
